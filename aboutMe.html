About Me

Hey there! I'm Jess, and I call the lovely town of Trinidad, CO, my home sweet home. It's a cozy little spot nestled among the breathtaking Rocky Mountains and vast front range, and I couldn't imagine living anywhere else.

My place is like a little zoo - four lively cats and one adorable pup make sure there's never a dull moment! They're my furry companions and best friends, always keeping me company and reminding me to appreciate the simple joys in life.

One thing you should know about me: I'm an absolute nature enthusiast! Whether I'm hitting the trails for a hike, paddling down a serene river in my kayak, or just enjoying a leisurely stroll through the woods, I feel most alive when I'm surrounded by Mother Nature's beauty.

But don't get me wrong - I'm just as happy spending quality time with my loved ones. Whether we're planning a fun-filled family BBQ or enjoying a laid-back movie night with friends, those moments are what make life truly special.

When I'm not out exploring or hanging out with my crew, you'll probably find me curled up with a good book, trying out a new recipe in the kitchen, or tinkering away on a creative project. I believe there's always something new to learn and discover!

If you're up for a chat or just want to connect, feel free to reach out to me on LinkedIn. I'm always down for meeting new people and swapping stories!
